BUT - Budget under Travel

App idea under the theme "Travel"

This was my first own school project what I created through out my studies at the unservistiy of Berkeley Ext. We had to come up with our own travel app idea, beginning from user research, to competitor checks, to user interviews and design plus testing. BuT is designed for created for travlers with a time periode of 2-3 weeks, mostly Road or Round-Trips. You can add your whole trip in the application and your planned travel budget, what will always be calculated for your best and cheapest way. Father on you can look up realated trips and recommendation from other travelers.

AssistMe

Application for homeschooling and scheduling

AssistMe is an app designed for parents whose children are partaking in online learning. This all-in-one app organizes crucial information, communication and schedules in a way that is accessible to all family members. Parents can check with an enable notification system if their kids checked in to class and when they are done, or even set them a reminder. Kids on the otherside can also check whatelse is scheduled throughout their day, like acitvities or important appointments.
