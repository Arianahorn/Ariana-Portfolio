Values

Creativity

“A creative mind is an active mind” - this quote couldn’t describe myself better.
During an activity (mostly when I do one of my favourite outdoor sports) I get the most out of myself, what I always try to give back in my work. Sketching was from a young age of mine already something that truely made me happy and is now again in my work life a high accomblished skill from my co-workers. Creativity is so much more though, I am happy when people tell me they love my way of thinking and what I do/create with it.

Entrepreneurial

Follow the path that already exists is a good approach when you want a secure and easy life, but establishing something new and earn the value of it is my goal. Think outside the box and you will gain more knowledge and confidence. Seeking for opportunities to grow on was and will be always my biggest trait.

Adventurer

Traveling fulfills myself, even though we live in a global pandemic right now doesn’t keep me off from exploring my environment and closest surrounding. Finding the needs of each individiual and adjust them with others is the most challenging work. Being adventurous gives me information to understand those needs.
